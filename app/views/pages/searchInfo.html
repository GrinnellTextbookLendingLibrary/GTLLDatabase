Search Information

Borrower Beware

Note that our search function is not as robust as commercial companies. Take care with spelling and punctuation.

What if I am not getting any results?

  1. Check your spelling.
    • Is the title spelled correctly?
    • Is the name(s) of the author(s) spelled correctly?
  2. Try searching for only one of the authors/only by title.

What can I search for?

  • Case-insensitive text match from title
    e.g. "ART OF WAR" and "art of war" return same results
  • Case-insensitive text match from authors, broken by spaces/punctuation
    e.g. "richard, morris" and "Morris Richard" and "morris, richard" return same results
  • Both title and author(s)
