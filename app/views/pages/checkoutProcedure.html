Procedure for Borrowing Books

Materials to Bring

  • Your Student ID
  • Printed-out booklist (instructions below in the section titled "Printing Your Booklist")

    You MUST bring a pweb booklist in order to checkout books; no other booklist will be accepted.

  • Lots of patience

Procedure

Come to the library during our open hours with the materials specified above. When you arrive, please be prepared to wait in line. You will receive a place card with your position in line, and we will call people based on that card. Those at the back of the line can leave and return later if necessary. So, if you have card 49, and we are working with the person who received card 12, you are free to leave and return later. However, if you fail to return before your card is called, the next person in line will be called.

When your card is called, you will come to the door of the lending library and hand us your booklist. You will read and fill out a form regarding your intent to borrow textbooks for the semester (link to a sample form is at the end of this section) while we look to see which books of those you need are available. We will keep the form as well as your printed booklist.

Printing Your Booklist

  1. Log in to Pioneer Web.
  2. Scroll down the page and click on “Pictorial Class Lists & Book Lists” on the left side.
  3. Click on “View your book list based on your current courses and reserve texts” under the list of your courses.

    Note: if you are taking a course that has not yet been added to your schedule on P-Web, print out a page containing the books for that course from the bookstore, and bring both that page and your syllabus in addition to your official booklist.

  4. Print the resulting page from the bookstore website.
